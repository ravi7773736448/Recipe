My Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2¼teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the Filling:

  • ⅔ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1½ tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar,eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into a lareg boel and cover with palstic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours
  6. After the dough has doubles in size ,roll it out into large rectangle
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon
  8. Spread the filling onto dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll
  9. Cut the roll into 3cm sctions and place flat into baking tray
  10. Prehaet the oven to 350F or 180C,then bake the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brown
